Sockets that carry atomic messages across various transports like:

  • in-process
  • inter-process (shared memory)
  • TCP
  • multicast

You can connect sockets N-to-N with patterns like

  • fan-out, fan-in
  • publish-subscribe
  • task distribution
  • request-reply
